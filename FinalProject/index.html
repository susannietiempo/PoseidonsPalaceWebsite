Our Story

Poseidon's Palace is a locally-owned store in the Greater Moncton area that is bringing Monctonians and its visitors a taste of the Maritimes! The store offers a variety of seafood and souvenirs that are sourced from local artisans.

The shop was opened in 2007 by Saul T. Crack. Since then, it has always strived to provide the freshest seafood the local community. We take great pride in sourcing all of our seafood from local fishermen, from the wharf in Alma to the piers in Murry Beach, we only buy the freshest catches ...
