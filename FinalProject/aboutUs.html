Our Story

Poseidon's Palace is a locally-owned in the Greater Moncton area that is bringing Monctonians and its visitors a taste of the Maritimes! The store offers a variety of seafood and souvenirs that are sourced from local artisans.

The shop was opened in 2007 by Saul T. Crack. Since then, it has always strived to provide the freshest seafood to the local community. We take great pride in sourcing all of our seafood from local fishermen, from the wharf in Alma to the piers in Murry Beach, we only buy the freshest catches.

In 2019 Saul passed the reins over to his son Jimmy Cracks, who has decided to take the little store into the 21st Century with the addition of this website. Jimmy also decided to expand slightly and has opened a small souvenir section in the shop to try and attract the tourist market.

Our Mission



To provide the freshest possible Seafood to Moncton and the surrounding area.

Our Vision



To bring Moncton local seafood culture to everyone!
